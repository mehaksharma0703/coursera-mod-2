Three monuments of India

Taj Mahal, Agra is a beautiful white marble Monument on the banks of the holy river Yamuna in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

Mysore Palace, Mysore.The Palace of Mysore is the most famous historical monument, located in the city of Mysore in Karnataka and one of the most visited monuments in India.

Harmandir Sahib, Amritsar.The Golden Temple is known as Harmandir Sahib is a Sikh Gurdwara located in the Amritsar, Punjab.
